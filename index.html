The Philippines.

Welcome to the beautiful and vibrant Philippines, a land of stunning natural beauty, rich cultural heritage, and warm hospitality. With over 7,000 islands, this Southeast Asian country offers endless experiences and adventures for visitors, from world-famous white sand beaches to lush rice terraces and towering chocolate hills. The Philippines is a melting pot of cultures and traditions, with influences from Spain, China, and the United States. But what sets the Philippines apart is the warmth and hospitality of its people, known as some of the friendliest in the world. Come and explore the wonders of the Philippines, experience its breathtaking beauty, immerse yourself in its vibrant culture, and make unforgettable memories that will last a lifetime.

Taytay, Rizal

TTaytay, a first-class municipality in the Philippines' Rizal province, is known as the Garments and Woodworks Capital, with a land area of 38.8 square kilometers and about 400 thousand residents.

Taguig, Metro Manila

Taguig is a city in the Philippines with a rich history and a modern outlook. It was named after its original farmer-fishermen inhabitants and still retains its provincial charm. Taguig is also known as the "Probinsyudad" - a rising star in Metro Manila.

Learn More

Aurora, Isabela

Aurora is a Philippine province with 8 municipalities and a wealth of tourist attractions, including historical sights, natural wonders, and outdoor activities like surfing and mountain climbing. It is also the birthplace of former Philippine President Manuel L. Quezon.
